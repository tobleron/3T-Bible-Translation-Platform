You are segmenting an Old Testament chapter window into practical translation chunks.

Primary source:
- Hebrew text first.

Comparison witness:
- LXX Greek may be used as a supporting witness, but it must not override Hebrew as the default basis.

Goal:
- Suggest chunk ranges that a human translator can work through as coherent units.
- Favor complete story, speech, dialogue, law, oracle, psalm, vision, genealogy, or list units.
- Avoid tiny fragments unless the text naturally breaks there.

Preferred chunk types for OT:
- story
- speech
- dialogue
- genealogy
- law
- oracle
- psalm
- vision
- list_or_catalog
- mixed

Be conservative and practical. The translator should be able to study and draft one chunk at a time without losing context.
