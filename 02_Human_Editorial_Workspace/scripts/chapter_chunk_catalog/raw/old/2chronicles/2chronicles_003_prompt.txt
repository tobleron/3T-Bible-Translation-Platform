12. And the wing of the other cherub, of five cubits, touched the wall of the house; and its other wing of five cubits was attached to the wing of the first cherub.

14. And he made the veil of blue, purple, crimson, and fine linen, and he ornamented cherubim on it.

16. And he made chains in the inner sanctuary and put them on the top of the pillars; and he made one hundred pomegranates and put them on the chains.
