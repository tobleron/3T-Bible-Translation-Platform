5. For Yahweh your God has chosen him and his sons from all your tribes, to stand to minister in the name of Yahweh all the days.

8. They shall eat equal portions, except what they receive from the sale of their fathers’ estates.
9. “When you enter the land which Yahweh your God gives you, you shall not learn to imitate the abominations of those nations.

11. or one who is an enchanter or a medium or a spiritist or one who inquires of the dead.

21. Now you may say in your heart, ‘How will we know the word which Yahweh has not spoken?’
